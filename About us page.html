    About Us

    Atek Studio is a woodcraft company founded by Mr. Amit Tyagi and his team of passionate artisans. Their mission is to transform ordinary wood into extraordinary pieces of art, preserving its natural beauty while infusing it with craftsmanship and dedication. With years of experience and a deep love for woodworking, they approach each project with unwavering commitment to quality. Atek Studio's creations go beyond mere woodcraft, telling stories, from custom-made furniture to unique wooden decor pieces that spark conversations. They take pride in the details and appreciate the beauty found in imperfections, making each piece one-of-a-kind. Visitors are invited to explore their woodcraft collection and experience the artistry that defines Atek Studio.
